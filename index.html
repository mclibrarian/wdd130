Virginia

Virginia is a state in the eastern United States. It was the 10th state to ratify the Constitution and has the oldest permanent English settlement in North America. Its capital is Richmond and its geography includes mountains, forests, beaches, rivers, waterfalls, and caves. One of its most famous cities is Williamsburg, the original capital city and currently home to Colonial Williamsburg (the largest living history museum in the world), Busch Gardens, and the College of William and Mary. It is known for its rich history starting in the 1600s and continuing to today.
